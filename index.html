About the Smart Stick

The M-CLAP Smart Stick is equipped with advanced features, including ultrasonic sensors, audio navigation, a magnesium or aluminum base, and additional tools for convenience. It enhances mobility and independence for visually impaired and elderly users, prioritizing safety and ease of use.
